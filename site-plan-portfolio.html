Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to build revenue and share information by helping visitors learn about our white water rafting adventures, understand what trips we offer, and feel confident booking an outdoor experience with us. The site will showcase exciting rafting trips, beautiful scenery, safety-focused service, pricing or booking information, and clear ways for customers to contact us or reserve a trip.

Audience

The target audience is adventure-seeking adults, families, students, and tourists who enjoy outdoor recreation and want a safe, exciting rafting experience. They need clear trip details, prices, safety information, photos, and an easy way to book. Most visitors will use a mobile device, but some may use a laptop or desktop when planning with a group.
